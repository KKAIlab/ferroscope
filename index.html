What counts as ferroptosis evidence?

BODIPY 581/591 C11 oxidation, MDA, 4-HNE, altered GPX4 abundance, mitochondrial morphology or partial Ferrostatin-1 rescue are each insufficient on their own.

  1. DeathShow actual loss of cell viability or membrane integrity over time.
  2. DependenceConnect death to iron and lipid-radical chemistry with orthogonal rescue.
  3. MechanismUse genetics, target engagement and direct lipid or iron measurements.
  4. PhysiologyTest tissue, organoid, animal or human material without overstating translation.

DATA FRESHNESS

Source status

Each source is reported on its own. When one fails, the records it last returned are kept, marked stale, and published together with the date of the last success and the class of the error; the other sources are unaffected. If the retained records are more than 14 days old, that source publishes nothing and validation fails rather than presenting an old dataset as current.

Freshness is carried by the discovery route, not by the card. One study can arrive through a laboratory watch and through the general literature query. If one of those routes fails, only that route's copy is retained and the card is marked as having one retained route while the route that succeeded still backs it. A card is labelled retained only when every automated route that could refresh it has failed and no curated record supplies it.
